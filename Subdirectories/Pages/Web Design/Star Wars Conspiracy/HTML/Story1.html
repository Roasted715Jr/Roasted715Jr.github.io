Darth Binks is actually Padme's cousin-in-law. He got his lightsaber out of a lake on Naboo because a Jedi accidentally dropped in the water while fishing. He was innocent at the time, until proven guilty by the Jedi Council. Darth Binks was then sent to prison for eternity.

One day, another sith lord freed Darth Binks in hopes of a successful apprentice. He ultimately failed, explaining why Darth Vader exists now and why we never see Jar Jar.
