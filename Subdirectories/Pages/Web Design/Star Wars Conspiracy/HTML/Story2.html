Yeah, we all saw Han fall down into the Starkiller Base, then we saw the base get DESTROYED, so it was safe to say that we can go home and say, "Look, Mom, no Hans!"

But, if you recall, Luke Skywalker lived after Darth Vader said, "Luke, I am your father," Luke screams, "NOOOOOO!!!" then he falls down Cloud City onto an antenna, then rescued by the Milenium Falcon. Why couldn't this happen for Han Solo? There was a whole lightsaber duel on Starkiller Base before it got cracked in half and destroyed. I don't really believe in this, but it's an idea.
