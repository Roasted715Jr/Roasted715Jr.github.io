What education will I need?

In order to be a network and computer systems administrator, you may want to get one of these titles:

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Associate's degree
  • Post-secondary certificate (awarded for training completed after high school)

Of course, you may want to aim to get the higher level degrees as it will most likely increase your chance of being hired.

Learn more about the degrees at http://education.seattlepi.com/difference-between-masters-bachelors-doctorate-degree-completion-1722.html
